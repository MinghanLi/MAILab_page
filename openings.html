PhD Recruitment

PhD recruitment in the Computer Science Program and Robotics Program at Colorado School of Mines.

PhD students can expect hands-on research guidance, close support toward publishing in top conferences and journals, access to GPU resources, and opportunities to build exchange or internship experience with top universities and industry partners.

Interested applicants must apply through the Google Form . This is the only application route.

Master's and Visiting Students

MAI Lab is open to mentoring master's students and visiting students who are excited about visual intelligence, physical AI, and engineered systems.

Visiting students and master's students can expect close day-to-day mentorship, project ownership, publication-oriented research training, and exposure to broader academic and industry networks.

Interested applicants must apply through the Google Form . This is the only application route.

Collaboration on Papers and Funding

MAI Lab is open to collaboration on papers, datasets, benchmarks, grants, and funding opportunities.

We welcome collaborators from academia, medicine, and industry working on visual intelligence, medical AI, physical AI, and engineered systems.
